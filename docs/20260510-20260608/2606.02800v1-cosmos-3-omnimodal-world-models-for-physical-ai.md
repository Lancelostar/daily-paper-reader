我们推出了Cosmos 3，一个全模态世界模型系列，设计用于在统一的混合Transformer架构中联合处理并生成语言、图像、视频、音频和动作序列。通过支持高度灵活的输入-输出配置，Cosmos 3无缝统一了物理AI的关键模态——有效地将视觉-语言模型、视频生成器、世界模拟器和世界-动作模型整合到一个框架中。我们的评估表明，Cosmos 3在一系列多样化的理解与生成任务上达到了新的最先进水平，展示了全模态世界模型作为具身智能体可扩展、通用骨干网络的潜力。我们的后训练Cosmos 3模型被Artificial Analysis评为最佳开源文生图和图生视频模型，并在撰写技术报告时被RoboArena评为最佳策略模型。为了加速物理AI领域的开放研究与应用部署，我们在Linux基金会的OpenMDW-1.1许可证下（https://openmdw.ai/license/1-1/）开源了我们的代码、模型检查点、精选合成数据集和评估基准，获取地址为https://github.com/nvidia/cosmos、https://huggingface.co/collections/nvidia/cosmos3。项目网站为https://research.nvidia.com/labs/cosmos-lab/cosmos3。

## Abstract
We introduce Cosmos 3, a family of omnimodal world models designed to jointly process and generate language, image, video, audio, and action sequences within a unified mixture-of-transformers architecture. By supporting highly flexible input-output configurations, Cosmos 3 seamlessly unifies critical modalities for Physical AI -- effectively subsuming vision-language models, video generators, world simulators, and world-action models into a single framework. Our evaluation demonstrates that Cosmos 3 establishes a new state-of-the-art across a diverse suite of understanding and generation tasks, demonstrating omnimodal world models as scalable, general-purpose backbones for embodied agents. Our post-trained Cosmos 3 models were ranked as the best open-source Text-to-Image and Image-to-Video models by Artificial Analysis, and the best policy model by RoboArena at the time the technical report was written.
